Our Mission

A common issue in today's world is that many people find
it too hard to stay focused while attending or taking online
classes. Hence our team has come up with a solution that we
hope will be useful in combatting this issue.

How It works

We made a music app. Music helps calm people down as well as focus. Students can listen to peaceful, calming music while studying to help them focus. There is also an inbuilt alarm that goes off every hour telling you to take a short break! In our second app, you can control a character and walk around a world entering different rooms. The rooms have a proximity chat system which means that the closer you are to someone the better you can hear them hence reducing chaos of people talking over each other. You can share resources, ask each other questions and more using this app.
